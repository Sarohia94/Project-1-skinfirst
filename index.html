About Us

We want to promote skin wellness. Your skin is the body's largest organ and does a good job protecting us from the elements, disease and infections. So, it is imperative you take care of it!

Our face is the most noticeable part of the body, it is also the most exposed to the elements. Facial skin is sensitive, thinner and rich in pores, so special attention is required to look after it. We aim to strip down the basics of skin care and provide you with some useful information.

Fun Facts!

  • Changes in your skin can sometimes signal changes in your overall health.
  • Your skin has at least five different types of receptors that respond to pain and touch.
  • Some of the nerves in your skin are connected to muscles instead of the brain, sending signals (through the spinal cord) to react more quickly to heat, pain, etc.
  • Your skin is home to more than 1,000 species of bacteria.
